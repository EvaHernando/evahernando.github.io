Post-pc-only world

May 2012- July 2012

Client confidential. Experientia.

Context


Our client wanted to understand what was the future like in terms of devices people would use for their work and more specifically the role of the tablet on that future. To do that, we conducted user research in different countries.

I was part of the team who conducted in-depth interviews and a PD workshop In Italy. I also help to design the workshop that took place to present preliminary results to the client.








What we did


User research and in-depth analysis of the behaviors of people who have partially or largely shifted to mobile devices (tablets in particular) for both their professional and personal use in key markets in Italy, the USA and Taiwan. , was a research about the transition to a post-pc-only world. We carried out an analysis of the behaviors of people who have partially or largely shifted to mobile devices (tablets in particular) for both their professional and personal use in key markets in Europe (Milan, Italy), the USA (New York) and Taiwan (Taipei). The aim was to inspire the client’s thinking on future evolutions of computing devices.18 people (6 people in each location), who frequently use the tablet for both content consumption and creation, participated in contextual interviews and participatory design workshops. As part of the data analysis, we also hosted a 3-day, collaborative, data review workshop with the research teams and the client.By analyzing common behaviors in technology ecosystems, we created a process diagram of core activities people do on mobile devices, and identified enablers and pain points. We identified recurrent themes, and insights for each theme, describing users’ key behaviors. The ideas which emerged from the participatory workshops were also organized into these themes.
